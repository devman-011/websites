01 · Before the chair

A short note, a quiet phone call, a kettle on.

Every new patient begins with a fifteen-minute conversation — on the phone or in our front room — before any examination is booked. We'd rather know what you're hoping for than guess from a form.

02 · Behind the glass door · 60 quiet minutes

One chair. No rushing. No radios.

Intraoral scan. Photographs. A slow, honest conversation about what we'd recommend and, more importantly, what we'd leave alone. You leave with a printed plan — not a quote ambushing you at reception.

03 · Six months on

Hygienist recall, scan overlay, steady ground.

Your second visit is a hygienist appointment and a new scan, overlaid on the first. The software shows what's changed, what hasn't, and what — if anything — needs a quiet adjustment before the year is out.

Our approach

Scan. Plan. Place.

01 · Scan

Scan, never moulds.

We haven't taken a putty impression in eight years. Every mouth that sits in our chair is captured with a Trios 5 intraoral scanner — three minutes, no gagging, a millimetre-true digital twin on screen before you've finished your water.

  • Trios 5 wireless scanner · 20 µm accuracy
  • CBCT on-site for implant & endo cases
  • Scans shared with you, not kept in a drawer
02 · Plan

Plan in the open.

We walk the plan through with you at the 27-inch monitor next to the chair. Invisalign ClinCheck, DSD mock-up, implant guide — whatever is being proposed, you see the trajectory before anyone commits. Plans change. We redraw.

  • ClinCheck walkthrough & second opinion
  • DSD mock-up printed chair-side in resin
  • Written quote, itemised, e-mailed same day
03 · Place

Place, then recall.

Treatment runs on a cadence we set together — weekly, monthly, or eight weeks apart. Composite bonding is hand-sculpted across a single unbroken morning. Implants are guided from the planned file. Then the hygienist, always, six months on.

  • Composite bonding · one chair-morning
  • Guided implant placement from CBCT
  • Hygienist recall at 26 weeks, scheduled on exit
