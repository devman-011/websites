01 · Assessment

Sixty attentive minutes.

A proper orthopaedic examination — history, movement, load, palpation, special tests. We write down what we find, and what we don't. Imaging only follows a clinical question.

02 · MSK ultrasound

The picture beneath.

Dynamic, same-room imaging of tendon, ligament and joint — moving in real time. MRI when the depth or geometry demands it. No images ordered for their own sake.

03 · Return to play

Step by measured step.

Progressive loading, periodised strength, sport-specific conditioning, pitch-side testing before green-lighting return. Outcomes we can name: 2,107 resolved cases and counting.

Our approach

Examine. Image. Iterate.

01 · EXAMINE

Examine.

Sixty minutes, phone down. History, observation, joint range, provocation tests, functional screens. We name the clinical question before reaching for a probe.

  • · Written working diagnosis & differentials
  • · Red-flag screen · safeguarding if required
  • · Photographic range-of-motion record
02 · IMAGE

Image.

MSK ultrasound in the same room, same appointment. MRI booked typically inside five working days via our imaging partner. Only when the examination demands it.

  • · Dynamic MSK US · tendon · ligament · joint
  • · 1.5T & 3T MRI · sub-5 day turnaround
  • · US-guided diagnostic & therapeutic injection
03 · ITERATE

Iterate.

Treatment is rarely one-and-done. We schedule measurable checkpoints, adjust load, review imaging, and decide on surgical pathways together — only when conservative care is exhausted.

  • · Progressive, periodised loading
  • · Objective reassessment at 2 / 6 / 12 weeks
  • · Pitch-side testing before return-to-play
