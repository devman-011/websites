What we look after

Obstetrics, gynaecology,
and the years between.

Six strands of care under one named consultant. We schedule for an hour, not fifteen minutes — so the conversation finishes where it needs to.

01

Antenatal Care

Consultant-led · 8wk → 40wk

A booking appointment, twelve- and twenty-week scans, Harmony NIPT if you want it, growth reviews from twenty-eight weeks, and a birth plan written with you, not at you.

  • Booking bloods, Harmony NIPT
  • Growth scans at 28, 32, 36 weeks
  • Written birth plan by week 34
02

Prenatal Imaging

Anomaly & growth scans

Your consultant holds the probe — not a sonographer you've never met. Dating at 7–10 weeks, the detailed anatomy scan at twenty, growth and well-being from twenty-eight onwards.

  • Dating & viability
  • 20-week anomaly scan
  • Doppler & fetal well-being
03

Gynaecology

Menstrual · menopause · fertility

Heavy or irregular periods, fibroids, endometriosis, early fertility work-up, and the full menopause conversation — HRT, bone density, sleep, and everything that comes with it.

  • HMB, fibroids, endometriosis
  • Fertility workup & hormone panel
  • Peri- & post-menopausal HRT
04

Postnatal & Sixth-Week

At clinic or at home

A proper review at six weeks — physical recovery, contraception if you want it, pelvic-floor referral, and a frank conversation about how you are actually doing. Home visits across Chelsea and Kensington.

  • Scar & perineal review
  • Pelvic floor & physio referral
  • Contraception & return to work
05

Birth Planning

Chelsea & Westminster · Kensington Wing

We deliver at the Kensington Wing and at Chelsea & Westminster. Your consultant is on the rota — ninety-four percent of our patients are delivered by the person they booked with.

  • Birth preference document
  • Elective LSCS · planned VBAC
  • On-call from 37 weeks
06

Women's Health Clinic

Well-woman · smears · HPV

Annual well-woman reviews, cervical smears and HPV testing, contraceptive fitting and removal, and a proper hour to talk about the things you've been putting off.

  • Cervical smear & HPV co-test
  • Coil & implant fit/remove
  • Pelvic ultrasound in-clinic
01 · Your first appointment

Sixty unrushed minutes.

A full history, a proper examination, and time for the questions you wrote down on your phone at three in the morning. No clock on the wall, no nurse hurrying you along.

02 · The scan

Your practitioner holds the probe.

Not a sonographer you've never met. The clinician reading your scan is the one deciding your care — which means fewer hand-overs, fewer misreads, and a quieter room.

03 · Afterward

A direct line, not a switchboard.

You leave with your consultant's direct number. Questions at eleven on a Tuesday night get answered by the person who saw you on Monday — not a triage nurse reading from a protocol.

Our approach

Three small commitments
we actually keep.

01

Sit with you.

An hour at booking. Forty minutes at every routine antenatal. No timer, no nurse knocking on the door. If it matters to you, it matters here.

  • 60-minute first appointments
  • 40-minute routine reviews
  • Partner welcome at every visit
02

See the whole picture.

Pregnancy does not happen in isolation from sleep, work, mental health, and the relationship sitting in the chair next to yours. We ask about all of it, on purpose.

  • Integrated mental-health review
  • Pelvic-health physio on-site Thu
  • Registered dietitian referral
03

Stay after.

The six-week review is the beginning, not the end. Lactation, contraception, pelvic floor, return-to-work — we are a phone call away for as long as you need us.

  • Direct consultant number, 7 days
  • Postnatal home visits in SW3
  • Twelve-month open door

Your named consultant is on-call for you from thirty-seven weeks, and we have a small covering rota of the same three clinicians for the rare occasions she is unavailable.

  • Yes, across SW3, SW1, SW10, and SW7 — Chelsea, Knightsbridge, South Kensington, and Belgravia. The first postnatal visit is typically within seventy-two hours of discharge. Further afield, we arrange a home visit from a partner midwife and follow up at the clinic at week six.

  • Our standard antenatal package — full care from booking to sixth-week review, including every scan, blood test, and consultant-attended delivery — is £14,800 for 2026. High-risk, twin, and bespoke packages are quoted separately. A full breakdown is sent before you book; there are no incidentals.
